3D Whole-Body Grasp Synthesis with Directional Controllability (CWGrasp)

Georgios Paschalidis, Romana Wilschut, Dimitrije Antić, Omid Taheri, Dimitrios Tzionas
March, 2025
Cite Code Video arXiv Code Video 3DV 2025
Teaser: Directionally Controllable Whole‐Body Grasps
Type
1
Publication
In International Conference on 3D Vision (3DV) 2025

Abstract

We introduce CWGrasp, a framework for synthesizing realistic 3D whole-body grasps controlled by a sampled reaching direction. Our method first builds a ReachingField via ray-casting around the object, sampling collision-free direction vectors. We then condition both CReach (body) and CGrasp (hand) generators on this direction for initial synthesis, followed by a lightweight optimization stage to resolve penetrations. CWGrasp handles left and right hands, runs ∼16× faster than prior work, and outperforms baselines on GRAB and ReplicaGrasp.

Teaser

CWGrasp synthesizes controllable, whole‐body grasps by integrating geometry‐based reasoning early in the pipeline.

Method Overview

We sample a direction from ReachingField, generate a body (CReach) and hand (CGrasp) satisfying it, then optimize jointly to eliminate penetrations.
