About Me

Hello!

I am a PostDoc researcher in Perceiving Systems at the MPI for Intelligent Systems, where I completed my Ph.D. under the guidance of Professor Michael J. Black and Dr. Dimitrios Tzionas. My research focuses on generating Virtual Humans that move and interact with 3D scenes and objects like Real Humans.

My research interests are broad and include precise motion capture (MoCap) using multimodal sensors (IMUs, Cameras, Pressure Sensors, etc.), 3D reconstruction from images, Human-Scene Interaction, and 3D representation. Recently, I’ve expanded my interests to include cutting-edge topics in AI such as diffusion models and Large Language Models (LLMs) for object interaction.

Feel free to contact me about my research, collaborations, or anything else here.
